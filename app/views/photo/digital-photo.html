Go to a photo booth

You'll need to choose to get a photo with a code. In the passport application, simply enter the code to access your photo, and add it to your application.

Go to a photo shop

Get a digital copy of your photo, to upload to your application. You can also ask for a photo with a code.

Take a digital photo during your application

You'll need someone to help you take a photo using a digital camera, smartphone or tablet.
