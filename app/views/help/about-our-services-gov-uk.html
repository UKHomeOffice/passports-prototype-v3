Current passport processing times in the UK

How to apply

Apply online to use standard service.

You can also apply with a paper form from a Post Office, but it costs £9.50 extra and make it take longer to get your new passport.

If you need a passport urgently, you can find out more about the Online Premium or 1 week Fast Track service.

Urgent travel for compassionate reasons

If you need a passport to travel urgently for medical treatment, or because a friend or family member is seriously ill or has died, call the passport adviceline.
