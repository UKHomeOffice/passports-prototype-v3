Passport application processing times

Demand for passports is likely to be high as travel restrictions ease. There may be delays and passport applications could take up to 10 weeks. Check current processing times regularly for updates.

How long your passport application takes depends on what you're applying for and which service you use.

There are different turnaround times if you're applying from another country.

Urgent travel for compassionate reasons

If you need a passport to travel urgently for medical treatment, or because a friend or family member is seriously ill or has died, call the passport adviceline.
