Online Premium

You’ll need to book an appointment at a passport office.

Take your old passport and any documents to your appointment to be checked. You’ll need to return 2 hours later to collect your new passport.

What it costs

It costs:

  • £177 (or £187 or a 50 page frequent traveller passport) for an appointment within the next 5 working days
  • £142 (or £152 or a 50 page frequent traveller passport) for an appointment between 5 working days and 3 weeks

Apply, book an appointment and pay

To use the Online Premium service you’ll need:

  • your old passport
  • a digital photo of you
  • a credit or debit card

What to bring to your appointment

You need to bring your old passport.

You can ask someone else to go to your appointment for you. They’ll need to bring:

  • your old passport and any documents
  • a signed and dated letter from you, naming them and giving them permission to collect the passport
  • their ID or example a passport or driving licence

Changing your appointment

Call the passport adviceline to reschedule your appointment.
