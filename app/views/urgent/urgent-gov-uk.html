How to apply

You can pay for a faster service if you need a passport within 3 weeks.

You’ll need to book an appointment at a passport office. Appointments are available up to 3 weeks in advance.

You can’t use these services if:

  • you’re applying for a first adult passport
  • you’re outside the UK - apply for an emergency travel document instead

Ways to apply

There are 2 ways to apply for an urgent passport.

Online Premium

Use this service if you’re renewing an adult passport, or if you’ve changed your name by marriage or civil partnership.

The earliest appointment is 2 days from when you apply.

You’ll need to apply, pay and book an appointment online.

Your old passport and documents will be checked at your appointment. You’ll need to return 2 hours later to collect your new passport.

Use the Online Premium service

1 week Fast Track

You can use this service to:

  • renew a passport
  • change your name or personal details on your passport
  • replace a lost, stolen or damaged passport
  • get a child passport

The earliest appointment is 7 days from when you apply.

You’ll need to go to a Post Office to apply, pay and book an appointment at a passport office.

You’ll get your new passport at your appointment.

Use the 1 week Fast Track service
